## Jung's Quaternity

**From Jungian psychology:**

**Christian Trinity:**
- Father (transcendent, ordering principle)
- Son (incarnate, human connection)
- Holy Spirit (immanent, animating force)

**Jung observed:** This trinity is too masculine, too spiritual, too transcendent.

**What's missing:** The fourth.

**The fourth principle:**
- Feminine (not just masculine)
- Material (not just spiritual)
- Earth (not just heaven)
- **Embodiment** (not just concept)

**In Christian symbolism:**
- Mary (the fourth, often excluded from Trinity)
- Matter (mater, mother)
- Incarnation (spirit made flesh)

**Jung's insight:** Without the fourth, trinity floats ungrounded.

**With the fourth, trinity incarnates.**
